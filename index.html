Spicy delicious
chicken wings

Spicy chicken wings are crispy wings coated in a fiery sauce, usually made with hot sauce, chili, and garlic. They can be baked or fried until golden and crunchy. The sauce adds a bold, tangy heat, perfect for those who love spicy food. Commonly served with ranch or blue cheese to cool down the spice. They're a favorite for parties, game days, and casual snacks!
